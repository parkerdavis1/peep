# About peep

**peep** is a simple web app for editing bird vocalization recordings for submission to the Macaulay Library at the Cornell Lab of Ornithology.

It is designed to be a fast and easy way to perform the most common editing tasks:
- Trimming the beginning and end of the recording
- Applying a high-pass filter to remove low-frequency noise
- Normalizing the volume

The app is written in Svelte and TypeScript. It is open source and the code is available on [GitHub](https://github.com/parker-davis/peep).

## Privacy

All processing is done in your browser. Your audio files are never uploaded to a server.
